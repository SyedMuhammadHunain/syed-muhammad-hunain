NED University CS Student | Full Stack Dev | Startup Founder
------------------------------------------------------------

My development journey began in 2022, though I briefly paused to prioritize securing my admission to my dream program at NED. I translated my learning into action by engineering real-world applications, competing in high-intensity hackathons, and delivering production-grade web solutions for my startups. Today, I actively apply this expertise as the Co-founder of Corelogex, focusing on scalable architectures, AI integration, and providing web solutions.
